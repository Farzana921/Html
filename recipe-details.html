Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix ingredients together in a bowl.
  3. Scoop onto a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden.
